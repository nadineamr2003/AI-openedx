How StudyPath works

A quick guide to the adaptive quiz flow and the support you may see along the way.

1. Your quiz adapts as you answer

StudyPath adjusts topic choice, difficulty, and support based on your answers, confidence, timing, and recent progress.

2. Choose the right practice mode

Normal Practice balances growth across selected topics. Weakness Review focuses on shaky areas. Challenge stretches stronger areas when the selected lecture scope is ready.

3. Feedback is structured around learning

After each answer, you receive a short explanation, a mastery update, and a recommended next step such as continuing, reviewing, or trying a guided step.

4. Guided recovery appears when support is useful

If the system detects meaningful struggle, it may offer a worked example before giving you one focused recovery question.

5. Your dashboard changes perspective

The dashboard adapts to the selected course. It may emphasize repair, growth, or stretch potential depending on your recent progress.

Confidence prompts are optional

You can ignore optional confidence prompts, but using them helps the system choose better support.

Accessibility support

Use Read Aloud on any explanation to listen to the current feedback. This does not affect your score or progress.
